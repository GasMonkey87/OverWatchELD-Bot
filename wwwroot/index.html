OverWatch ELD Hub is the central website for drivers and managers. Log in with Discord to access your VTC portal, dispatch, and company tools.

What this website is for

Drivers can sign in with Discord to access their company portal. Managers can use the same hub to handle dispatch, announcements, driver profiles, and website settings.

How login works

After logging in, the system checks what supported VTCs your Discord account belongs to. If one VTC is found you are sent there automatically. If multiple are found, you can choose which one to enter.
